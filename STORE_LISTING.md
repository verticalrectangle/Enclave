## Description

```
Enclave is a live remote for your coding agent. Run oh-my-pi (omp) on your Mac or
Linux box, share the session, and drive it from your phone: watch every turn stream
in, prompt and steer the agent, answer its questions, or stop it — from anywhere.

Not a chatbot. Enclave connects to a real coding agent running on your own machine.
The code runs where your code lives; your phone is just the control surface.

SEALED END TO END
Every frame is sealed on your device with AES-256-GCM. The relay only ever sees
ciphertext — never your keys, your prompts, or your code. A view link joins
read-only; a full link can drive.

WATCH IT WORK
• Live transcript — messages, tool calls and results, streaming in real time.
• Activity — the whole subagent swarm, each with its tools, tokens and cost, and a
  tap into any subagent's own transcript.
• Trust — an honest, read-only mirror of the session: model, context, participants,
  relay, and the seal.
• Live Activity + Dynamic Island — the current turn on your lock screen, and a nudge
  when the agent is waiting on your answer.

ONE PASTE TO JOIN
Run omp /collab on the box, paste the link, and you're in. No account. No sign-up.
No setup.

Enclave is a client for oh-my-pi — you'll need omp running on your own machine to
use it.
```
